The Studio

Craft passed
from hand
to hand

BLADE is not just a haircut. It is a ritual rooted in the finest traditions of classic barbering, reinterpreted through a contemporary lens. Every session is a dialogue between the barber's eye and the client's identity.

Each master at BLADE has trained at leading schools in London and New York. We work exclusively with hand-crafted tools and professional-grade grooming products from the world's best brands.
